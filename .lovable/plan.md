## Design & Technical Details
- **Color palette**: Dark background (#0a0a0f), dark blue accents, white text, electric blue/cyan (#00d4ff) for highlights and CTAs
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif, good hierarchy with large headings
- **Animations**: Fade-in on scroll, hover scale effects, smooth section transitions
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first, adapts gracefully to all screen sizes
- **SEO**: Proper heading hierarchy, meta descriptions, semantic HTML structure
- **No backend needed**: Fully static, frontend-only site
